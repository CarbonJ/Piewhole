Piewhole

A very simple food diary...

Sometimes tracking calories is overkill or just plain tedious. Maybe you just want to know that you're eating right. Maybe you have the habit of just popping food in your craw and not thinking about it.

Piewhole hopefully will help you keep what your doing in your mind, allowing you to make better food choices. Maybe, just maybe, you'll shut your piewhole.
